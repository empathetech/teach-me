About

What this is, and what it isn't.

I'm Chris Ling — these are curricula I've built to teach myself things that mattered enough to want to keep around. They live here as a portfolio: navigable, citable, durable.

Each course is designed by me with Claude Code as a collaborative writing and engineering partner. I do the framing, scope, voice, and design calls; Claude drafts prose and writes the SPA's interaction logic under that direction. Every factual claim in a lesson is cited and linkable — see the 📚 Sources section at the bottom of any lesson, and the per-course Credits page for the full bibliography.
